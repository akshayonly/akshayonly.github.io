Hello World, I'm Akshay Shirsath.
I joined Computer Science for my bachelor's but after a year decided to switch the field and completing my bachelor's in Microbiology.
I stumble upon Bioinformatics accidentally after watching a TED talk. I thought - "There's a field which uses the principles and methods based on computer science but to understand and solve biological problems. I need to know more."
During my master's we were briefly introduced to Machine learning and neural networks. Fascinated and curious to understand more in-depth, I decided to learn Machine learning and Deep learning. So here I am still learning new insights into this amazing field and now eager to write about topics that sit between AI and Biology.
